Delete Your Account

You can delete your Bago account at any time. This will permanently remove your profile, shipment history, and all associated data.

From the app

  • 1 Open the Bago app and sign in to your account.
  • 2 Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
  • 3 Scroll down and tap Settings.
  • 4 Tap Delete Account and confirm your choice.

Can't access the app?

Send us an email from the address linked to your account and we will process the deletion within 30 days.

Email us to delete account
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Any active shipments or pending payouts should be resolved before deletion. Balances will be forfeited upon account removal.
